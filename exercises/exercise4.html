Client Site Brainstorming

Idea #1: UMass Life Hacks

For this client site, I plan on creating a webpage for UMass students, faculty, and visitors of the university to turn to as a source for unique tips and advice that is provided by current UMass students and/or alumni.

This webpage would include a directory to "life hack" categories that will include:

  • On Campus Living
  • Off Campus Living
  • Dining
  • Du Bois Down-Low
  • Hidden Gemss

Idea #2: UMass Co-Ops

For this client site, I plan on creating a directory for all UMass affiliated co-ops that currently operate. As of right now, there is a webpage for each of these co-ops indivudally. However, it could be beneficial for both students searching for these co-ops, as well as for the student-run businesses themselves, if there was a general page that helped more conveniently direct people to all of the student businesses on campus.

This webpage will contain:

  • Home Page
    • "What is a Co-Op?"
    • "How to Get Involved"
  • Menu/Directory
    • Greeno Sub Shop (Central)
    • Sweets and More (Orchard Hill)
    • Sylvan Snack Bar (Sylvan)
    • People's Market
    • Earthfoods Cafe
    • Bike Coop
  • Photo Gallery
  • FAQ

Idea #3: Button Bento

For this client site, I plan on creating a webpage for my coworker's arts and crafts business, Button Bento. This webpage would include:

  • Home Page

  • Menu/Directory
    • Custom Orders
    • Events
    • Photo Gallery
