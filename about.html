About Me

My name is Sabrina Taylor, and I am currently a junior undergraduate student at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am originally from Los Angeles, California. I am pursuing a degree in English with a specialization in Professional Writing and Technical Communication (PWTC), as well as a minor in Information Technology (IT). After I finish school, I plan on pursuing a career in technical writing and/or user experience (UX). Over the last few years, I have gained experience with Python, HTML/CSS, MadCap Flare, Microsoft Word/Excel, Adobe Photoshop/InDesign/Lightroom, and Stencyl. When I am not writing essays or documentation, I love swimming, playing videogames on my Nintendo Switch, photographing, and hosting my radio show on WMUA 91.1 FM (Keep Bouncing on Thursdays 1-2:30 PM, tune in)!
